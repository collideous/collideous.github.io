My Photos Across The Web

Patrick August 12, 2025

Cycling photos of mine have been used on various websites over the years, sometimes with my permission, sometimes without. I don't mind either way. If someone has the courtesy to ask, I gladly give my blessing. If not, it's okay too. Searching my name or nickname on Google Images quickly reveals such usage. Here's a partial list of examples.

1. MTBR used "Twannberg here I come."

2. Wheel Brothers used "Headless conquistador."

3. Bike Rumor used "My 44 Kid Dangerous."

4. The Inner Ring used "Waiting for Le Tour de France."

5. Ironman used "Plan A was the Chasseral."

6. Bike Magazine used "The good old days."

7. The Active Times used "Sunny winter trail."

8. The Ontarion used "First snow."

9. Uncover Colorado used "My Osprey Raptor 10 backpack."

10. Outside Magazine used "A bit of singletrail."

11. Volagi used "Climbing the Col de l'Aiguillon."

12. Veloccino used "Back in business."

13. Fat-Bike.com used "Winter is back."

14. Adnan Mahmud used "Rays of sunlight."

15. Objevim used "Lots of singletrail."

16. SWITZERlanding used "Minus ten degrees."

17. Chan-Bike used "A spectacular day."

Curious, are you occasionally checking where your photos appear?
