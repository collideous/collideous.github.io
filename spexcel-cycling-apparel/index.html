Spexcel Cycling Apparel

Patrick

Other than a review on theBidon blog and a few Reddits, there's very little online about Shenzhen-based cycling apparel brand Spexcel. I first ran across Spexcel on AliExpress in 2023. I bought a few windproof vests and two bibs for the fall and winter seasons. The quality and workmanship was outstanding. In 2024, I revisited their AliExpress store multiple times. I bought a half dozen jerseys, several bib shorts, and a thin, windproof vest. I have a drawer full of jerseys, some old, some newer, what I was missing were short-sleeve jerseys made from extremely lightweight materials for hot summer days. By hot I mean 24-28°C. No jersey in the world would make cycling fun at 30°C. Spexcel had just what I needed.

I'm 176cm, with normal proportions, muscular rather than skinny. At the moment, I have a bit more fat around my waist than I would normally have. I buy the bib shorts in medium and the jerseys in large. Once I can slim back to my normal weight, the jerseys should still be good. The vests in size large are a little tight and will fit perfectly once I drop a few kilos.

What's to like:

  • excellent construction
  • comfortable high-end fabrics
  • stylish colors (zippers in the same color)
  • small unobtrusive logos
  • unbeatable value for money

If you're looking for comfortable, well-made performance clothing that doesn't cost an arm and a leg, check them out. Riding on a tight budget that keeps the wife happy and leaves some spare dollars for your kid's college fund or your retirement has never been easier.
